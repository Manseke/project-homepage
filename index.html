Hero Bunker

A Strategic Superhero Role-Playing Experience

Explore the Project

What is Hero Bunker?

Hero Bunker is a digital role-playing superhero game inspired by the board game Bunker. Players take on the roles of unique superheroes placed in a global crisis scenario, where only a limited number of people can survive inside a bunker.

Each player is given hidden traits, abilities, and backstory elements. Through discussion, strategy, and decision-making, players must decide who deserves to survive.

This creates a highly interactive experience that combines storytelling, critical thinking, and social interaction.

Why Does This Project Exist?

Many modern digital games focus mainly on fast-paced action and repetitive mechanics. While entertaining, they often lack depth in communication and strategic thinking.

Hero Bunker was created to solve this problem by encouraging players to communicate, debate, and think critically. It transforms a physical board game concept into a digital experience that is more accessible and engaging for today’s audience.

The game also develops important skills such as teamwork, reasoning, and decision-making, making it both entertaining and educational.

How the Game Works

1. Character Assignment

Each player receives a superhero with unique abilities and a hidden identity.

2. Hidden Information

Players are given secret traits that influence their chances of survival.

3. Discussion Phase

Players debate and analyze who should enter the bunker.

4. Decision & Outcome

The system evaluates choices and determines survival results.

Sponsor Interview

Sponsor: Ahmet Daulet (Educator)

Perspective

Our sponsor explained that role-playing and decision-making games are highly valuable in education because they develop responsibility, critical thinking, and teamwork skills.

Why This Project Matters

According to the sponsor, the game addresses important real-world skills such as decision-making under pressure, understanding different roles in society, and recognizing that success depends on cooperation, not just individual strength.

Educational Value

The project becomes valuable if players improve their argumentation skills, learn to respect different opinions, and engage in meaningful discussions.

Target Audience

The game is best suited for teenagers who are capable of analyzing situations and making logical, well-reasoned decisions.

Key Requirements

  • Clear and simple rules
  • Balanced superhero abilities
  • Logical and fair decision-making system

Important Considerations

  • Avoid overly complex mechanics
  • Ensure respectful interaction between players
  • Keep the project realistic and achievable

Final Advice

The sponsor emphasized that the game must balance entertainment with educational value. A clear purpose and structured design are essential for success.
